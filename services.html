Ways to Engage

Flexible Ways to
Work Together

Every organization is at a different stage. Choose the model that fits your timeline, budget, and the depth of support you need.

Short-Term

Project-Based Consulting

A focused, fixed-scope engagement to solve a specific challenge or deliver a defined outcome — without a long-term commitment.

  • Defined scope & deliverables agreed upfront
  • Strategy, communications plans, program design, and process improvement
  • Facilitation, stakeholder engagement, and special initiatives
  • Workshops, training sessions, and executive briefings
  • Clear start and end dates with milestone check-ins

Best for: A discrete initiative, an event or launch, a one-time deliverable, or testing the fit before a larger engagement.

Pricing: Fixed project fee or hourly rate, starting at $125/hour.

Long-Term

Embedded & Fractional Partnership

An ongoing relationship where I work as an extension of your team — building, evolving, and stewarding the work that keeps your organization moving forward.

  • Retainer or base-pay arrangement for predictable support
  • Standing capacity across strategy, communications, and operations
  • Continuous improvement and program ownership
  • Priority availability and a dedicated planning cadence
  • Knowledge transfer that builds your internal capability

Best for: Organizations building lasting capacity, growing teams, or carrying sustained, mission-critical work.
