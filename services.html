Engagement Models

Flexible Ways to
Engage

Every organization is at a different stage. Choose the model that fits your timeline, budget, and the depth of support you need.

Short-Term

Project-Based Consulting

A focused, fixed-scope engagement to solve a specific challenge or deliver a defined outcome — without a long-term commitment.

  • Defined scope & deliverables agreed upfront
  • Risk assessments, process maps, SOPs, and governance frameworks
  • Audit-readiness reviews and controls testing
  • Workshops, training sessions, and executive briefings
  • Clear start and end dates with milestone check-ins

Best for: Discrete initiatives, audits, one-time deliverables, or testing the fit before a larger engagement.

Pricing: Fixed project fee or hourly rate, starting at $125/hour.

Long-Term

Embedded Partnership

An ongoing relationship where I work as an extension of your team — building, maintaining, and evolving the systems that keep your organization running.

  • Retainer or base-pay arrangement for predictable support
  • Standing capacity across risk, operations, and governance
  • Continuous process improvement and program ownership
  • Priority availability and dedicated planning cadence
  • Knowledge transfer that builds your internal capability

Best for: Organizations building lasting infrastructure, ongoing compliance programs, or sustained transformation.

Pricing: Monthly retainer or base compensation, scoped to your needs. Contact now →
